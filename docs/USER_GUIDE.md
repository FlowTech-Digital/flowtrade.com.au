## Getting Started

### Account Setup

1. Visit [flowtrade.com.au](https://flowtrade.com.au)
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your business details
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete onboarding wizard

### Business Profile

Configure your business settings:

- Business name and ABN
- Contact information
- Service areas (postcodes)
- Trade categories
- Hourly rates and pricing

## Smart Quoting

### Overview

Generate accurate quotes 75% faster with AI-powered calculations.

### Creating a Quote

1. **Select Customer**: Choose existing or create new
2. **Define Scope**: Describe the job requirements
3. **Add Line Items**: Materials, labor, equipment
4. **AI Assistance**: Let FlowTrade suggest pricing based on:
   - Historical job data
   - Material costs
   - Travel time
   - Complexity factors
5. **Review & Send**: Finalize and email to customer

### Quote Templates

Save time with reusable templates for common jobs:

- Bathroom renovations
- Electrical inspections
- HVAC installations
- Plumbing repairs

### Quote Status Tracking

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Draft | Quote in progress |
| Sent | Awaiting customer response |
| Accepted | Customer approved |
| Declined | Customer rejected |
| Expired | Past validity date |

## Route Optimization

### Overview

Reduce travel time by 30% with intelligent route planning.

### Daily Scheduling

1. View jobs for the day
2. Click "Optimize Route"
3. FlowTrade calculates the most efficient order
4. Accounts for:
   - Traffic patterns
   - Job duration estimates
   - Customer availability windows
   - Fuel efficiency

### Map View

Visualize your day with interactive maps:

- Job locations pinned
- Optimal route highlighted
- Real-time traffic updates
- Turn-by-turn navigation links

### Multi-Technician Scheduling

For businesses with multiple technicians:

- Assign jobs by skill set
- Balance workloads
- Minimize total fleet travel
- Handle last-minute changes
